Innovators are made, not born.

Our Mission

BU Spark! is an initiative to support student-driven innovation in computer science, engineering, and related disciplines.





Join a Project

In a prestigious university setting like Boston University, there is no shortage of good ideas. Through BU Spark!, you can engage with fellow students to take their innovations to the next level or complete projects on behalf of an external partner.



1. Spark! X-Lab

Sometimes, the best way to learn is through experience. BU Spark! is driven to provide you a comprehensive, hands-on approach in pursuing technology projects. BU Spark! hosts experiential learning courses that are designed to match you with a project and teach you the skills necessary to see that project come to life.

1. Fellowship Program

Do you want to pursue a project of your own? If so, BU Spark!’s Fellowship could help you and your team jumpstart the development of your ideas. The program aims to provide you with a collaborative co-working space, access to mentors, and skill building to help ignite your ideas. A limited number of paid internships are available for the Spark! Fellowship, so sign up today!
