Programs

BU Spark! Fellowship

The Spark! Fellowship is dedicated to catalyzing Boston University student innovators who are passionate about solving a problem (or creating an opportunity) and committed to building a technological solution. The program aims to provide students a collaborative co-working space in which they can build out their solution. Look below, for the resources we provide each of our students participating in the Fellowship program.

Apply

One-on-One Mentorship

Recieve a mentor with the expertise you need to help you bring your ideas to life.

Co-Working Space

Get priority access to our co-working space with coffee, snacks, beanie bags, standing desks and more located in the BU Spark! office.

Semester Stipend

Receive up to $1,500 per team each semester to obtain the necessary resources to pursue your own project.
