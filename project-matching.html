Programs

Project Matching Website

The Spark! Fellowship is dedicated to catalyzing Boston University student innovators who are passionate about solving a problem (or creating an opportunity) and committed to building a technological solution. The program aims to provide students a collaborative co-working space in which they can build out their solution.

One-on-One Mentorship

Projects are typically focused on one technical skill. A student team typically works for 50-80 hours over the course of a 6-8 week period as part of a class assignment. Technical focus determined by the class assignment and varies by semester

Co-Working Space

Scope and technical focus driven by project partner. Past projects range from app development, evaluation of machine learning models, and creating a image filtering microservice.

Semester Stipend

"Sumbit a project that can be project on in 24 hours. Help us provide prizes and event costs for sponsored hackathons, such as BostonHacks, Hack the Gap, or SheHacks Boston."

The Spark! Fellowship is an internship program that will be held between September 18th to December 12th. Fellows will have access to the following: One-on-one mentoring, group activities with other members of the Spark! Innovation community, support to launch a crowdfunding project, and priority access to our collaborative coworking space (111 Cummington Mall, Boston, MA, 02215, Room 150A).
