Spark! Mentorship Program

BU Spark! is seeking mentors to assist student innovators in taking their project ideas to the next level! Spark! Innovation Mentors will guide motivated teams of students participating in BU Spark!’s innovation development programs. In these programs, students engage in a semester-long experience to build a technical product, which can be an app, algorithm, or another creative output. Students will benefit from your expertise to help them develop their ideas, make strategic decisions, and overcome technical barriers.
We have two mentoring levels so you can find an option that matches your schedule. Please see below, for descriptions of each of the opportunities.

Spark! Innovation Coach

Serves as the primary mentor for a student innovator or team throughout their product creation journey. Coaches are asked to check in with their teams on a weekly basis (in-person or virtually).

Spark! Innovation Specialist

Offers deep expertise in a particular topic, technology area, or programming language. Specialists are asked to make at least two hours per month (in-person or virtually) available to advise different student teams.

  • Not seek a formal business partnership with the students
  • Participate in a kick-off session (in person if you are local) and a final evaluation

We are holding mentees to the following standards:
