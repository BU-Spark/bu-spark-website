Programs

BU Spark! X-Lab

Sometimes, the best way to learn is through experience. BU Spark! is driven to provide you a comprehensive, hands-on approach in pursuing technology projects. BU Spark! hosts experiential learning courses that are designed to match you with a project and teach you the skills necessary to see that project come to life.

Class Project

The classes available this semester are machine learning, app development, and data science.

Subcribe to our newsletter for updates on which classes will be available this spring semester.

Consulting Project

BU Spark! recruits student-consulting teams to work on technical projects provided by companies or organizations in Boston.

Apply to be a part of our roster of students, for consulting opporunities.

Student Club Projects

BU Spark! also helps student clubs source projects, like the Global App Initiative.

We also sponsor hackathons held at Boston University, such as BostonHacks, Hack the Gap, or SheHacks Boston.
