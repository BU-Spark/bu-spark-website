CS491: BU Spark! Innovation Ventures

BU Spark! innovation ventures is a two-part course that provides students with an opportunity to advance a technology product or innovation as well as instruction on the fundamentals of innovation and product creation. This course requires students to have an innovation or product concept in mind or already in development. Look below, for the resources we provide each of our students enrolled in the course.
