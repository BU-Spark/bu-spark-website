Spark! Conference Room

A space for our community of innovators to meet with their teammates and mentors. Advanced level access to space is limited to individuals engaged formally in a Spark! program or through leaders on the Ignite Student

Spark! Innovator Co-Working Space

A coworking space in the BU Spark! office that our community of innovators and their team members can reserve to work on their projects. Spark! Innovators can access desk space, lockers, or reserve the conference room or lounge for a community event. You can apply on our website for access to this space!

Spark! Collaboratory

"Get to know our community of innovators and visit our collaboratory open from 9 am - 5 pm to all students. Hang out on a beanie bag and talk to other like-minded individuals about your emerging product ideas or the latest technology trends. For students formally participating in a BU Spark! Program, visit our resource page on our website to apply for the use of the office after hours as well as to reserve it for community events."




Request a Consultation

Spark! hosts a variety of consulting options to answer any questions you may have regarding your project. To request a consultation, fill out the form below to pick between our three consulting areas.

General Consultation

The classes available this semester are Machien Learning, App Development, and data science. Subscribe to our Newsletter for Updates on what classes are available in the Spring.

Technical Consultation

Scope and technical focus driven by project partner. Past projects range from app development, evaluation of machine learning models, and creating a image filtering microservice.

CS Mentorship Program

BU Spark! has partnered with individuals, companies, and organizations in the city of Boston to provide technical mentorship to Spark! innovators. Students who sign up to receive a mentor will be matched with a professional to assist in the development of their technology. Mentors can help with the following topic areas: app development, web development, software development and more!
